Short Question

Qno1:What is chemical equilibrium?

Chemical equilibrium:
A state of a chemical reaction in which forward and reverse reactions take place at the same rate is called chemical equilibrium.

Qno2:State the law of Mass Action.

Law of Mass Action:

It states that the rate at which a substance reacts is directly proportional to its active mass and the rate at which the reaction proceeds is directly proportional to the product of the active masses of the reactants.

Qno3:State conditions for equilibrium.

Conditions for Equilibrium:
  • Concentration of none of the reactants or products is changed.
  • Temperature of the system is kept constant.
  • Pressure or volume of the system is kept constant.
